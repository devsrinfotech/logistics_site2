FAQ?

Frequently Asked Questions

  • 01. How can I pay for your logistics services?

    Answer:

    In case of Advance booking customers have to pay a token amount via NEFT / IMPS / PAYTM for booking confirmation.

  • 02. How can I track my shipments?

    Answer:

    Our booking system is a simplified process. You can place a booking request online on our website/App or you can call our customer care number, Our team after verification finds the nearest truck for you.

  • 03. Do you provide Parcel, bike, courier or car transport service?

    Answer:

    We only provide full truck transport services, we do not provide services for courier, parcels or car transportation.

  • 04. Can I place a booking with multi-point pick-up and drop?

    Answer:

    You can definitely do so. However, extra costs for the extra point of delivery or pick-up will be levied accordingly. Our team will provide you with the additional cost incurred at the time of booking.
